Essential Grammar in Use - Raymond Murphy

Raymond Murphy offers an introduction to English grammar. A book written in easy to understand language. Murphy goes from the most elementary sentence structures and their particularities in the English language. The book has explanations, examples and a lot of exercises. Although the book is aimed at beginners, there are sections that are difficult to understand and require the help of a tutor. The book contains a CD with supplementary material.

Prepositions and adverbial particles- L.A. Hill

The encounter with this book is the result of dumpster diving of my great friend Pedro. Pedro knows that I am a collector of English grammar books. The book is a gem. The book explains the use, structure and meaning of each English preposition. Each entry offers the multiple uses of the preposition from its most frequent use to its most hermetic. In the text you can see hundreds of examples. Unfortunately, the book is aimed at an intermediate and advanced audience because it is necessary to know elementary and intermediate grammar in order to understand the explanations in the book.
